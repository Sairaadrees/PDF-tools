Privacy Policy

Last updated:28-Mar-2025

At Toolkit Pro, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our online tools and services.

TL;DR: We don't store your files permanently. Your uploaded images and files are processed temporarily and automatically deleted. We collect minimal data to improve our services.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you use Toolkit Pro, we may collect:

  • Email address (if you contact us or create an account)
  • Basic usage statistics and preferences

b. Non-Personal Information

We automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Pages visited and tools used

c. Files You Process

For our image and file tools:

  • Uploaded files are processed temporarily
  • Files are automatically deleted after processing
  • We don't store your original files permanently
  • We don't claim ownership of your files

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our tools and services
  • Improve user experience and develop new features
  • Monitor usage patterns and analyze trends
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Enhance security and prevent fraud

3. Data Retention

We retain different types of information for different periods:

  • Uploaded files: Deleted immediately after processing (typically within 1 hour)
  • Usage data: Retained for up to 12 months for analytics
  • Contact information: Retained as long as needed to respond to your inquiry

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze tool usage
  • Improve performance

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Analytics for usage statistics
  • Cloud hosting providers for file processing
  • Payment processors for premium features

These third parties have access to limited information needed to perform their functions.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to processing
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
